The Hero's Profile

About

CS undergrad turned AI builder. I train models, ship products, and learn best when something breaks at 2am.

Hey, I'm Saranya 👋

I'm a third year Computer Science student at Oregon State University with a focus in AI and a minor in Finance. I started in NLP research, spending over a year building and evaluating transformer based translation pipelines at Star Lab, and realized I wanted to build things that actually ship — not just things that score well on a benchmark.

So I started building. An LLM evaluation platform. A real time lecture tool that won a hackathon. A finance dashboard. Each project taught me something the classroom didn't — that the hard part isn't the model, it's everything around it.

Outside of code I work as an Academic Learning Assistant helping students navigate college, lead fundraising for the AI club on campus, and TA a databases course. I find it genuinely interesting that a few well chosen evaluation metrics can tell you more about a model than weeks of reading papers.
